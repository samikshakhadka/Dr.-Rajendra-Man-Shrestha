About Me

Who Am I?

Dr. Rajendra Man Shrestha is an Associate Professor at Padmakanya Multiple Campus, Kathmandu, Nepal, with extensive experience in teaching and research in the field of Statistics. His academic journey includes a PhD in Modeling for Assessing Impacts of Climate Change on River Hydrology, focusing on the Bagmati River basin in Nepal. His research interests include Statistical Data Analysis, Climate Modeling, and Biostatistics.

Dr. Shrestha has a passion for guiding students in research and thesis writing, and has contributed significantly to the academic community through his research, publications, and participation in national and international conferences.
